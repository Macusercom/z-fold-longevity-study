Crack Development Timeline — What to Expect
Year 1 (0–12 months)
8.1% crack rate — cracks are rare early on. Most users don't notice anything in the first year. The factory protector does its best work here.
Year 2 (13–24 months)
~20% crack rate — risk nearly triples. Protector peeling becomes more common, and wear begins to accumulate around the fold crease.
Beyond Year 2 (24+ months)
33.7% crack rate — one in three users see micro cracks. Fold 3 and 4 users are hit hardest; Fold 5+ shows far better durability at this stage.
05 — Usage Habits
Does How You Use It Matter?

Short answer: No. Usage habits have zero measurable impact on device durability. The age of the phone matters far more than how you use it.
